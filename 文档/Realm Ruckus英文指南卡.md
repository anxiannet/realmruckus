## Guide 1 — Setup & Goal

```text
SETUP & GOAL

WIN
Control all 3 Territories
in the same Realm.
Win immediately.

4 REALMS
The Bonewilds
The Emberlands
The Ironfang Range
The Webwood
3 Territories in each Realm

SETUP
1 Shuffle Monsters and Relics
  into the Main Deck
2 Deal 5 cards to each player
3 Shuffle the 12 Territories
4 Reveal 3 Wild Territories
5 Choose a starting player

WILD TERRITORIES
Keep 3 face up when possible.
Refill immediately after one
is claimed.
```

## Guide 2 — Turn & Actions

```text
TURN & ACTIONS

YOUR TURN
1 Draw 1 card
2 Take 1 main action
3 Resolve all effects
4 Discard down to 7 cards

If the Main Deck is empty,
skip the Draw step.

CHOOSE 1 ACTION

DEPLOY
Play 1 Monster to:
• an uncontrolled Wild Territory
• one of your Territories
  with fewer than 3 Defenders
Resolve its entry effect.

RAID
Send 1–3 Defenders from one
of your Territories to Raid
an opponent's Territory.

PLAY A RELIC
Play and resolve 1 Relic,
then discard it.

You may pass.
```

## Guide 3 — Raids & Combat

```text
RAIDS & COMBAT

RAID LIMITS
Raid only with Defenders
already in play.
Monsters cannot Raid
from your hand.
Unchosen Defenders stay behind.

STARS
Stars = Health = Damage

COMBAT
1 Attacker chooses attack order
2 Each attacker attacks once
3 Choose 1 defending Monster
4 Both deal damage at once
5 Defeat Monsters whose damage
  reaches their Stars

Defender damage can build up
across several attacks.

SUCCESS
No defender remains:
Surviving attackers move into
the Target Territory.

FAILURE
A defender remains:
Surviving attackers return to
the Origin Territory.

After combat, all survivors
fully recover.
```

## Guide 4 — Key Rules & Endgame

```text
KEY RULES & ENDGAME

DEFENDER LIMIT
Maximum 3 per Territory.

TERRITORY RESET
When a controlled Territory
loses its last Defender:
return it to the Territory Deck,
shuffle, then refill the Wild
Territories to 3 if possible.

RETURN TO HAND
The Monster is not Defeated.

CARD EFFECTS
Follow card text exactly.
If the required legal targets
do not exist, do not resolve it.
Choose the full number unless
the card says “up to.”

EMPTY MAIN DECK
After a full round with:
• no Monster or Relic played
• no Territory changing control
compare in order:
1 Territories
2 Total Defender Stars
3 Most Territories in one Realm
4 Number of Defenders
5 Still tied: draw
```

- Use bold section headers and short lines.

- Use icons only as reading aids; icons must not add rules.
